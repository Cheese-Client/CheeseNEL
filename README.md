## 🧀 Introduction / 项目简介

**CheeseNEL** is a lightweight Minecraft proxy that solves the problem of international clients being unable to connect to NetEase's Chinese Minecraft servers. Through intelligent protocol conversion and packet rewriting, CheeseNEL allows you to use any launcher (HMCL, PCL2, official launcher, etc.) to log in with your NetEase account and smoothly play on NetEase-exclusive minigames, rental servers, or survival servers.

**CheeseNEL** 是一款轻量级 Minecraft 代理工具，用于解决国际版客户端无法连接网易《我的世界》国服服务器的问题。通过智能协议转换与数据包重写，CheeseNEL 允许你使用任意启动器（HMCL、PCL2、官方启动器等）登录你的网易账号，并流畅游玩国服特色小游戏、租赁服或生存服务器。

> ⚠️ This tool is for technical research and learning purposes only. Do not use it to disrupt game balance or for commercial purposes. Ensure you own a NetEase account and comply with relevant terms of service.
>
> ⚠️ 本工具仅限技术研究与学习交流使用，请勿用于破坏游戏平衡或商业用途。使用前请确保你已拥有网易版账号并遵守相关服务条款。
